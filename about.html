About Laurent

Our story of passion, tradition, and authentic Italian cuisine

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi family, Laurent has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for nearly four decades. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into a beloved dining destination, while never losing sight of our commitment to traditional recipes and warm hospitality.

Our head chef, Marco Rossi, learned the art of Italian cooking from his grandmother in Tuscany. Every dish we serve carries the essence of those time-honored traditions, prepared with the same love and attention to detail that has been passed down through generations.

Our Philosophy

At Laurent, we believe that great food brings people together. We source only the finest ingredients, many imported directly from Italy, to ensure that every meal is an authentic experience. Our pasta is made fresh daily, our sauces are prepared from scratch, and our commitment to quality never wavers.
